KIDZ KAMP AFTER-SCHOOL DETAILS


Kidz Kamp will be opening on April 12, 2021. Open Monday - Thursday from 110pm to 6pm. (If you are in need of a full day of care on Friday's please let me know) We will provide after-school care for all Doyle children, regardless of whether they are participating in online only or hybrid instruction. (Do not hesitate to contact me with any questions) Operating predominantly as an outdoor program. We are currently awaiting City guidelines for indoor capacity restrictions as well as City approval for air purifiers/filters. We will have access to a room in Doyle Recreation Center to store student's belongings. In case of inclement weather we will be able to pivot into the gym and we will be following the strict County and CDC guidelines regarding indoor activities. Our goal is to focus on socialization and physical activity. Our program will offer arts and crafts, structured games, and free play.
