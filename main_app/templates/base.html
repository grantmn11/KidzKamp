Kidz Kamp

Established in 1996. Kidz Kamp is ingrained in the community, it has become a vital part of the neighborhood. Kidz Kamp prides itself on instilling kids with positive values, promoting good health, exercise, and respect for others. The high standard it holds its participants and staff to has helped make it one of the best after school and summer programs in the San Diego area.
